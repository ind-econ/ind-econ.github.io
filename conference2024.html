NIE Conference & PhD Symposium 2024

The Network of Industrial Economists Conference and PhD Symposium will be held on June 27 and 28, 2024 at the University of Warwick.
  • 27th June 2024 - PhD Symposium in Industrial Economics
  • 28th June 2024 - Network of Industrial Economists Conference

Warwick Business School and the Department of Economics are pleased to invite you to the PhD Symposium in Industrial Economics and/or the Network of Industrial Economists Conference.

Submissions of abstracts are invited for the 2024 PhD Symposium in Industrial Economics, taking place on Thursday 27th June 2024 at the University of Warwick.

The Symposium brings together early-career economists to foster discussion and dissemination of research in all areas of industrial economics. A number of external academics will join our internal faculty members to act as discussants, providing high-quality feedback. Financial assistance for presenters will be available to contribute towards travel and accommodation (if required). The extended abstract submitted for the symposium can refer to a finished paper, a thesis chapter, or a working draft. If available, a paper draft will be required before the conference. Abstracts must be submitted by June 1st, 2024 (23:59, GMT) via the following link.

The PhD Symposium is then followed by the Network of Industrial Economists (NIE) Conference, taking place on Friday 28th June 2024 at the University of Warwick. The NIE conference will consist of presentations by prominent industrial economists.

Participants can attend the Symposium and/or the Conference, but participants in the Symposium are encouraged to attend the Conference the following day.

Both events have NO registration fees but registration for the events will be required. Lunch and refreshments will be provided to all participants, for both events.

Important Dates:

  • Deadline for submitting a paper to the PhD Symposium: 1st June 2024
  • Acceptance decisions: 14th June 2024
  • Registration to attend both events: 24th June 2024
  • PhD Symposium in Industrial Economics: 27th June 2024
  • Network of Industrial Economists Annual Conference: 28th June 2024

Please feel free to forward this call to all interested researchers and colleagues, including graduate students who would like to participate and contribute to both events.
