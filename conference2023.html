NIE Conference & PhD Symposium 2023

The Network of Industrial Economists PhD Symposium and the Conference took place at the University of Leicester School of Business on June 8 and 9th, 2023, respectively.

PhD Symposium

PhD students and the early career researchers in the Industrial Economics presented their fascinating researches on June 8, 2023. Particular congratulations to Jinlin Wei from the University of Warwick, who was awarded the Best Paper prize of the Business and Industry Section of the Royal Statistical Society.

NIE Conference

The NIE Conference was held on June 9, 2023. Leading scholars in the Industrial Economics presented their recent studies.
